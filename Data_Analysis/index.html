The Job Analysis of Data Analysts in 2018

Source: zhipin.com, one of the most popular internet recruitment websites.

The Salary of Data Analysts

Generally speaking, the salary of data analysts is just like other IT positions, such as software engineers.

There is an ever-increasing demand for data analysts in top-tier cities, such as Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing.

Requirements focus on having at least one-year work experience, holding a bachelor degree or higher.

The average salary is around 10,000 yuan per month. The mode is 15,000 yuan per month.

Educational Background of Data Analysts

Mostly, the background qualifications usually require at least a computer-related or statistically relevant bachelor's degree

Higher education, the greater the salary gap. The mid-range salary of a master's degree background candidate is higher than the high-range salary of a bachelor's degree background candidate.

70% job posts requests at least bachelor degree.

Work Experience of Data Analysts

In the job market, most companies are seeking for candidates that have at least 1-3 years work experience.

41% job positions require at least 1-3 years work experience while 32% require at least 3 - 5 years work experience.

The high-range salary for experienced data analysts can touch more than 30,000 yuan per month.

Job Descriptions of Data Analysts

In short, the most frequently mentioned are communication skills and data mining techniques.

SQL, Python and R top the lists of requirements, followed by Hadoop, SAS, SPSS, BI, EXCEL, Spark and Hive.

From the word cloud, we can see that the candidates should be sensitive to data, seek valuable insights from the information.

Tools & Prospect

To sum up, the tools used in the data collection process and data analysis are as follows.

  • Data Collection: Python(requests + xpath)
  • Data Storage: MySQL
  • Data Analysis: Pandas, matplotlib, numpy, scipy
  • Data Visualization: WordCloud, jieba

Difficulties encountered and solutions

  • Anti-spider(Forbidden IP): Use dynamic IPs from IP pools maintained by a server
  • Anti-spider(Too frequent access): Control the request frequencies, use the sleep function.
  • Anti-spider(Verify code): Proactively initiate a request to the captcha page.

Furture plans

  • Selenium: Try selenium, web driver automation
  • Redis: Try redis distributed spider
  • Scrapy, MAP API integrate: Read the documentation of Scrapy
  • Node.js Mongoose, Echart: Use the node.js, mongoose,Echart to do similar works
  • Collecting data: Collecting data of different cities and different jobs from different websites.
