The Job Analysis of Data Analysts in 2018

Source: zhipin.com, one of the most popular internet recruiting websites.

The Salary of Data Analysts

Generally speaking, the salary of data analysts is just like other IT positions, such as software engineers.

There is an ever-increasing demand for data analysts in top-lier cities, such as Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing

Requirements focus on having at least one year experience, holding a bachelor degree or higher.

The average salary is around 10,000 yuan per month.The payroll is 15,000 yuan per month.

Educational Background of Data Analysts

Mostly, the educational qualifications usually require at least a computer-related or statistically relevant bachelor's degree

Higher education, the greater the salary gap. The salary-mid for a master is higher the salary-high of a bachelor.

There are 70% posts requests at least bachelor degree

Work Experience of Data Analysts

In the job market, most companies are seeking for candidates that have at least 1-3 years work experience.

41% posts request at least 1-3years work experience and 32% posts request at least 3-5years work experience

The salary-high for experienced data analysts can touch more than 30,000 yuan per month.

Job Descriptions of Data Analysts

In a nutshell, the communication skills, data mining skills are most frequently mentioned.

As for the technical skills, SQL, Python, R, Hadoop, SAS, SPSS, BI, EXCEL, Spark, Hive top the lists of requirements

From the word cloud, we can see that the candidates should be sensitive to data, seek valuable insights into the information.

Tools & Prospect

To sum up, the tools used in the data collection process and data analysis are as follows.

  • Data Collection: Python(requests + xpath)
  • Data Storage: MySQL
  • Data Analysis: Pandas, matplotlib, numpy, scipy
  • Data Visualization: WordCloud, jieba

Difficulites encounted and solutions

  • Anti-spider-Forbidden IP: Using dynamic IP from IP pools maintained by a server
  • Anti-spider-Too frequent access: Controll the request frequencies, using sleep function
  • Anti-spider-Verify code: Proactively initiate a request to the captcha page
